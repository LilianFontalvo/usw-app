Niagara Falls

Location: Niagara Falls straddles the border between the United States and Canada, between New York and Ontario. Size: Horseshoe Falls is about 2,600 feet wide and 167 feet high, while American Falls is approximately 1,060 feet wide. Bridal Veil Falls is the smallest. Water Flow: On average, about 85,000 cubic feet of water per second flows over the falls, sourced from the Great Lakes. Conservation: Conservation efforts are in place to preserve the natural beauty of Niagara Falls and its surroundings.
